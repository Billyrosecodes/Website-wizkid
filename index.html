Top 5 songs

I honestly would say every single song by Coldplay is my favourite, I can't choose! "Nobody said it was easy" and I agree. Almost every song by Coldplay brings me joy and I can't say that there is one that I dislike. I tried my best picking out my favourite songs. Do enjoy.

5. Jaiye jaiye

This track makes me feel like I've had a double espresso with a side of silliness. I can't help but wiggle my hips to the beat and pretend I'm dancing like nobody's watching. The fusion of Afrobeat and Wizkid's playful energy in this song is absolutely infectious!

4. Fever

Fever is like a musical potion that turns up the heat on my mood meter. The sultry vibe combined with Wizkid's smooth vocals never fails to put a goofy grin on my face. It's the kind of song that makes you feel like you're in your own little world of romance.

Essence

Whenever I hear "Essence," I can't help but imagine myself on a beach with a goofy, carefree smile. The laid-back groove and the enchanting vocals by Wizkid and Tems create a magical atmosphere. It's like a mini-vacation for my ears!

2. Manya

Manya is like a one-way ticket to the party zone. The rhythm is so catchy that it's impossible not to move and groove to it. I enjoy cranking up the volume, letting loose, and pretending I'm a dance floor legend while listening to this track..

1. Daddy Yo

Wizkid's "Daddy Yo" is pure energy in audio form. Whenever it comes on, I can't help but transform into a goofy dance machine. The infectious beat and playful lyrics make it impossible to stay still. It's like a musical injection of adrenaline!
